Project Overview


Where is the wind project located?

Norfolk Wind Energy, LLC is developing a wind power project in Renville County in Minnesota.

How big is the wind power project?

  • Potential output capacity of 40-150 MW.
  • Could produce enough electricity to powerat least 12,000 average-sized American homes.

Who owns the Norfolk Wind Energy project?

Norfolk Wind Energy will follow National Renewable Solution's approach to wind energy development, which is different from more traditional “corporate developers” in the wind industry. The fundamental distinction is that we include the local community in the project by creating opportunities for them to be owners of Norfolk Wind Energy. We believe that landowners and the local community should share in both turbine leases and the potential revenues from a successful wind project.

Commercial Operation Date:

Norfolk Wind Energy expects to begin commercial operation in three to five years.
